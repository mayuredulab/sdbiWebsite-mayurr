Welcome to School of Data Science and Business Intelligence

Founded in 2018, SDBI is a premier school offering programs in Data Science, AI, ML, and Business Intelligence. SDBI in association with leading universities offers B.Sc. and M.Sc. Degree in Data Science and Business Analytics curated by top industry experts. We aim to achieve a perfect blend of theory, case studies, and hands-on practice for our students to bridge the gap between academics and industry needs.

Our courses are designed by industry leaders to prepare students for next generation skills like data analytics, data visualisation, machine learning, artificial intelligence, natural language processing, etc. making them job ready. We are driven by our pursuit of excellence in teaching, innovation, research, technology, and a continually evolving academic environment. Our campus is at the renowned Patkar Varde College, Mumbai accredited with A+ Grade by NAAC. All our degrees are offered under the prestigious University of Mumbai.

Bachelors

Bachelors in Data Science and Business Analytics

B.Sc. in Data science is a three years full-time undergraduate course that facilitates one to learn the industry-driven comprehensive curriculum at an early stage of his/her career.

Students learn, hone and apply their analytical and coding skills, collaborate in on a fast-paced atmosphere and learn from mentors coming from industry and academia.

  • Mandatory Internship
  • Industry connect sessions
  • 10+ Programming Tools and Languages
  • Capstone projects and 15+ case studies
View Course

Masters

Masters in Data Science and Business Analytics

M.Sc. in Data Science is a two years full-time postgraduate course that enables comprehensive learning with hands-on experience in solving real-life problems through interactive lectures, tutorials and practical coursework.

Students receive exposure to the latest tools and analytical methods to work with, derive insights from, deal with complex and unstructured data, and solve real-world problems.

  • Mandatory internships
  • Placement assistance
  • Mentorship of top data talent
  • Capstone projects and case studies
View Course

PG Diploma

PG Diploma in Data Science and Business Analytics

PG Diploma in Data Science is a one-year course meant for graduates and full-time working professionals to upskill themselves and become eligible for lucrative job prospects.

The course is structured around subjects such as statistical Computational methods, data analytics, machine learning and database management to help you build your career in advanced Analytics.

  • Diploma degree from MU
  • Gateway to international studies by completing 16 years of Education
  • 20+ Industry Projects
  • Dedicated Student Mentor
